Girls in Tech


Who We Are

Girls in Tech is an after-school club at Park City High School open to anyone. We are a group of 20 girls who get together every other week to learn new technologies by building projects.

Why should I join Girls in Tech?

Computing is where the jobs are â€” and where they will be in the future, but fewer than 1 in 5 computer science graduates are women. We aim to change that. Girls in Tech's mission is to close the gender gap in technology by exposing girls to computer science and giving them skills they need to be comfortable in whatever career they pursue.

Also, it is fun.

Do I need to have any experience to join?

No experience is necessary to join Girls in Tech.
